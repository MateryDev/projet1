This article presents a recipe for pan-fried red mullet, developed by chef Frédéric Greffet from the Trilance restaurant in Le Cannet. The dish is accompanied by peas, barigoule artichokes, gnocchi, Nice olives and savory. The recipe details the preparation of artichokes, cooking vegetables and fish, as well as making a creamy vegetable-based sauce. The chef suggests pairing this dish with a rosé wine.

This recipe is a dish based on "Lean" a Mediterranean fish which is from the sea bass family. It is cooked here with broccoli puree, candied tomatoes, piquillo peppers and pine nuts.

This dish is based on wild boar from local hunting, I cooked it when I arrived in Luxembourg. It is cooked with parsnips, butternut and a chocolate meat gravy.
